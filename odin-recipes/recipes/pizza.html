Veggie Lover's Pizza

Description

Why have pizza plain when you can make a slammin veggie pizza with 10x the flavor! This mouthwatering vegetarian pizza recipe features tomatoes, bell pepper, artichoke, spinach and more.

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Vegetables of your choice

Steps:

  1. Grab your dough and toss it in the air Dominoes style.
  2. Put your sauce, cheese and veggies on said dough and then put in oven, bake it.
  3. Enjoy your wonderful pizza in all its glory.
